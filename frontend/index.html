🔬 How Empathy Encryption Works

Privacy: AES-256 Fernet encryption makes the original text completely unreadable without the key.

Empathy: The EMOSIG prefix encodes a hashed emotional signature — AI systems can identify the emotion without reading the text.

Multi-emotion: Our NLP model detects multiple overlapping emotions with confidence scores, not just a single label.
